Account Deletion Request

We understand that you may want to delete your SundarGarments account. This page provides you with clear information about how to request account deletion and what happens to your data.

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, you will lose access to all your data, order history, and account benefits. Please consider this carefully before proceeding.

How to Request Account Deletion

Step 1: Send an email to our support team at support@sundargarments.com
Step 2: Include the following information in your email:
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Your registered email address
  • Your full name as it appears on your account
  • Confirmation that you want to permanently delete your account
Step 3: Our support team will verify your identity and process your request within 7-10 business days
Step 4: You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been successfully deleted

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed from our systems:

  • Account Information: Email address, password (hashed), profile details, preferences
  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, shipping addresses, billing information
  • Order History: Complete order records, purchase history, and transaction details
  • App Data: Wishlist items, saved preferences, app settings, and usage data
  • Communication Records: Support tickets, chat history, and customer service interactions
  • Authentication Data: Google Sign-In tokens and associated authentication information

Data Retention for Legal Compliance

Please note that certain data may be retained for legal and financial compliance purposes, even after account deletion:

  • Financial Records: Transaction records and payment information may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and accounting purposes
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained if required by law or for legal proceedings
  • Fraud Prevention: Limited transaction data may be retained to prevent fraud and ensure platform security

This retained data will be anonymized and will not be associated with your personal identity.

Alternative Options

Before deleting your account, you might want to consider these alternatives:

  • Account Deactivation: Temporarily disable your account instead of permanent deletion
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your data before deletion
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust your privacy settings to limit data collection
  • Unsubscribe: Opt out of marketing communications while keeping your account
